About Me

The Developer

I am a self-taught game developer based in Auckland, New Zealand. I love Game Development because it allows me the freedom to create anything I want by combining my artistic creativity, programming aptitude, and imagination.


I've worked on 15+ projects alone and with others in: 2D, 3D, Puzzle, Platformer, FPS, RTS, and many more.

The Journey

My journey began in high school with HTML and CSS. I later delved into Android Studio and Java/Kotlin to build apps for NGOs.


Fascinated by creating applications, I transitioned into Unity Game Development, learning 3D modeling and C# to bring my own characters and worlds to life.
